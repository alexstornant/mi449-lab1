Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun in our solar system and is the only known celestial body to support life. It is the fifth-largest planet and has a diverse range of ecosystems, including oceans, mountains, forests, deserts, and more. Earth's atmosphere contains oxygen, essential for supporting various forms of life. The planet is characterized by its dynamic geology, with tectonic activity, earthquakes, and volcanic eruptions shaping its surface. It has a variety of climates, from polar ice caps to tropical rainforests. Earth is part of the Milky Way galaxy and orbits the Sun, along with other planets in our solar system.
